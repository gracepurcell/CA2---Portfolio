B.C.I.

Have you ever wanted to have the ability to move things with your mind? Unfortunately, telekinesis is still only a theory and has not yet and may never be proven. With the help of Emotiv and Arduino, my project will take us that step further!
Using a brain to computer interface and a car from a simple arduino kit and parralax created a mind controlled wheel chair prototype.

This project was for my thesis in my Ô¨Ånal year of college. I chose it because i found the topic massively interesting and found it would be a good project to do due to the area of Brain Computer interfaces to be quite experimental at this present time.
This project was created with the help of an Emotiv Epoc Headset, two of their free software packages available from their website, Processing, Arduino, and a Parrallax Board of Education Bot.

In order to do this i trained my thoughts with the Emotiv Epoc Headset and the software Xavier Control Panel. The electrical currents from beneath the scalp read by the sensors on the headset was then converted to an Open Sound Controlled (OSC) message and sent across to a sketch created in Processing.
After that the Processsing translated the OSC message to a value and sent across a serial portal to the sketch made with Arduino.

I then implemented the Arduino board to turn left and right depending on the OSC message sent across from the headset.
