B.C.I.

Have you ever wanted to have the ability to move things with your mind? Unfortunately, telekinesis is still only a theory and has not yet and may never be proven. With the help of Emotiv and Arduino, my project will take us that step further!
Using a brain to computer interface and a car from a simple arduino kit and parralax created a mind controlled wheel chair prototype.

This project was for my thesis in my ﬁnal year of college. I chose it because i found the topic massively interesting and found it would be a good project to do due to the area of Brain Computer interfaces to be quite experimental at this present time.
This project was created with the help of an Emotiv Epoc Headset, two of their free software packages

available fromtheir website, Processing, Arduino, and a Parrallax Board of Education Bot. In order to do this i trained my thoughts with the Emotiv Epoc Headset and the software Xavier Control Panel. The electrical currents from beneath the scalp read by the sensors on the headset was then converted to an OpenSound Controlled (OSC)

message and sent across to a sketch created in Processing.
After that the Processsing translated the OSC message to a value and sent across a serial portal to the sketch made with Arduino. I then implemented the Arduino board to turn left and right depending on the OSC message sent across from the headset.

SERVER REMOTE

Instead of using docket books to create breakdown and service reports and then having administrative employees enter the collected data into the server back in the office, why not get rid of the middle man and create an application that speaks directly to the sever in real time and save directly from the engineer.

During the summer of 2016, I left my part time job and set up shop with an internship in a small Irish company on the southside of Dublin City. They specialised in creating software named GoodMove, to manage small business like themselves when it came to appointments, invoicing and awaiting payments. Most of their business lay with companies that dealt with customers in person such as KN install solutions and Gas Services Limited.

With technology progressing they found it was time to upgrade their software and add new features. My job there was to create an android Remote application that communicated to and from the software of each company. This was not only good for the environment, helping ditch docket books, but it also helped prevent mistakes and show how or when they happened with time stamp and signatures. The system was implemented with security checks

and real time information using JDBC to communicate with the server. Unfortunately, as i was only in my 3rd of 4 years in college, when the summer came to an end i was unable to continue my work with that project. This however was one of my favourite projects to have taken on since i started my journey in college because it was all self taught and showed me what i was capable of when i put my mind to something.
